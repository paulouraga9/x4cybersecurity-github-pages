Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed & insured?

Absolutely! We are fully licensed & carry a $1,000,000 General Liability Policy, on top of being up-to-date on the latest professional techniques and industry standards.

How long does the service take?

Service time varies depending on the size of your home and number of windows. A typical residential service takes between 1-3 hours. Commercial projects may take longer depending on the scope. We'll provide you with a time estimate when we give you a quote.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It's not necessary for you to be home during the entire service, though we do recommend being present at the beginning to discuss any specific concerns and at the end to review the completed work. If you can't be home, we can make arrangements for access and payment.

What form of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, checks, and digital payment methods like Venmo and PayPal. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts for recurring service plans, senior citizens, and military personnel. We also have a referral program where you can earn credits toward future services by referring friends and family.
